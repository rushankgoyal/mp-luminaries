Aniket Verma lost his mother when he was three. His uncle Amit moved to Bhopal for work and brought young Aniket with him from Jhansi. The city became home. A bat became his way through the world.

Twenty-one years later, that Bhopal upbringing has shaped one of Indian cricket's most talked-about power-hitters. On 17 June, Verma walked out to bat for Bhopal Leopards — the defending Madhya Pradesh T20 League champions — and hit a **29-ball 60** to help the team chase down **195** against Malwa Stallions with more than an over to spare at Holkar Stadium, Indore.

## A Career Built on Sixes

Verma made his name at Bhopal Leopards in the 2024 MP League, scoring **273 runs in six innings at a strike rate of 195**, with his best knock a blazing **123 off 41 balls — 13 sixes**. Those numbers earned him a spot representing Madhya Pradesh in the Under-23 circuit, where he scored **101 not out off 75 balls** against Karnataka in the one-day tournament.

Sunrisers Hyderabad had seen enough. They bought him at his base price of **₹30 lakh** ahead of IPL 2025. In just his third game for SRH, he walked in at 25 for 3 and smashed **74 off 41 balls** against Delhi Capitals — six sixes. He went on to score **236 runs across 14 matches** that season at a **strike rate of 166**.

He carried that composure into IPL 2026, hitting **43 off 18 balls** in Sunrisers' opening match against Royal Challengers Bengaluru to help them post **201**. The critics who had wondered whether his power game could translate to the top level of T20 had their answer.

## Captain at Home

When the IPL ended, Verma returned to Bhopal as captain of the Leopards — the same franchise that first gave him a marquee stage. He has not let the armband slow him down. In one earlier MPL 2026 match he scored **85 not out off 39 deliveries**, and his 29-ball 60 on 17 June sealed another win as the Leopards defend their title.

Verma's uncle Amit pushed him towards cricket while holding down work to keep the family stable. Both of them now watch the results in Bhopal's floodlit arenas, where the young man they bet on is very much delivering on that bet.
