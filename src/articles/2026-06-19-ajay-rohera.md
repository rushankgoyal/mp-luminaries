On the morning of 8 December 2018, a 21-year-old from Dewas walked out to bat for Madhya Pradesh in his very first first-class match. By the time Ajay Rohera was finished, he had scored **267 not out off 345 balls** — **21 fours and five sixes** — the highest individual score on a first-class debut in Indian cricket, breaking a record that had stood for 25 years.

The previous mark was 260, set by Mumbai's Amol Mazumdar in 1994. Rohera's innings was so commanding that Madhya Pradesh declared at **562 for 4**, Hyderabad folded twice, and MP won by an innings. Rohera walked away with the Man of the Match award, the highest individual score in MP's first-class history, and a world record — all before his sophomore season had started.

## A Long Road Back

That debut belonged in a highlight reel. What followed was more complicated. After four seasons with Madhya Pradesh, Rohera did not get a state contract renewal and spent time rebuilding with Puducherry. He averaged over 47 in his first season there, proving himself again against different opposition.

The Madhya Pradesh T20 League gave him a route back near home, and he took it. Playing for Jabalpur Royal Lions in the 2026 Scindia Cup, Rohera has produced the kind of innings that recall the 21-year-old who demolished Hyderabad on a cold December morning in Bhopal. On 13 June, he scored **130 not out off 65 balls** — **nine fours and nine sixes** — the **highest individual innings of the 2026 MPL T20 season**. His knock lifted the Royal Lions to **205 for 5** and a 54-run win, their third successive victory.

Through five matches, Rohera has scored **268 runs at an average of 67 and a strike rate of 165**. His approach is direct: take responsibility, bat deep, let the score build around you — a method that produced a 345-ball masterpiece in 2018 and now generates floodlit destruction in Indore.

For Madhya Pradesh cricket, Ajay Rohera is both a reminder of what the state can produce and proof that a batter from Dewas who once rewrote Indian cricket's history books on debut has not finished writing his story.
